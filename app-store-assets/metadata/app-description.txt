iMe: Your Personal Life Assistant

iMe is a comprehensive personal assistant application designed to streamline your productivity, health tracking, and lifestyle management across web and mobile platforms.

FEATURES:

• DASHBOARD
Get a quick overview of your day with personalized summaries of your schedule, health metrics, and finances all in one place.

• SCHEDULE MANAGEMENT
Organize your calendar, set reminders, and manage events with an intuitive interface. Never miss an important appointment again.

• HEALTH TRACKING
Monitor your wellness journey by tracking steps, water intake, sleep patterns, and other health metrics. Set health goals and track your progress over time.

• FINANCE MANAGEMENT
Take control of your finances by tracking income, expenses, and budget goals. Get insights into your spending habits and save more effectively.

• DISCOVERY
Explore personalized recommendations for activities, health improvements, and financial strategies tailored specifically to your lifestyle.

• ACCESSIBILITY FEATURES
Control the app using voice commands and enjoy a variety of accessibility options including high contrast mode and font size adjustments.
